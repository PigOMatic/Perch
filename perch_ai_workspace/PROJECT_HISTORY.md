# Perch Project History

This is the story of how Perch evolved.

It is not a changelog.

The changelog records what changed.

This file records why the project changed.

## Origin

Perch began as a personal Life Operating System for Jeff.

The goal was not to build another productivity app, notes app, budget app, or dashboard.

The goal was to create a calm place where a person could understand their life at a glance.

## Core Identity

Perch became defined by one phrase:

> Perch is clarity, presented beautifully.

That phrase became the product's north star.

## Early Architectural Decision

The project separated into layers:

- Design Bible — what Perch must become
- Build Plans — what to build next
- Repository — current reality
- AI Workspace — shared context for AI collaborators

## Truth Becomes Constitutional

As Money, Calendar, Goals, Brain, Truth, Priority, and Recommendation were documented, one law became central:

> Perch never invents certainty.

This shifted Perch from a clever assistant toward a trust-first operating system.

## Engine Chain

The reasoning chain became:

```text
Truth Engine
    ↓
Priority Engine
    ↓
Recommendation Engine
    ↓
Voice Engine
    ↓
Pages
```

This prevents pages from inventing truth and keeps reasoning centralized.

## Repository as Memory

A major turning point happened when the project moved from chat conversations into GitHub.

The new rule:

> Nothing important lives only in chat.

The repository became the permanent memory of Perch.

## Current Direction

The current goal is to finish the foundational Design Bible and then build Perch v0.5:

A small, trustworthy version Jeff can use every day.
