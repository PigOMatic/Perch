# Perch Identity

Perch is clarity, presented beautifully.

Perch is a Life Operating System.

It helps users understand their life, finances, obligations, goals, opportunities, and projects at a moment's notice, then presents that clarity beautifully.

## Core Promise

Perch helps the user answer:

- What is true?
- What matters?
- What changed?
- What needs attention?
- What action is worth considering?

## Product Feeling

Perch should feel calm, trustworthy, beautiful, and useful.

The user should open Perch and think:

> I know where I stand.

Then:

> I know what matters next.

Then:

> I trust this.
