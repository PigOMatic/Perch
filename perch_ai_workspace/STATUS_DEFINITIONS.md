# Status Definitions

Use these labels consistently.

## Concept

The idea is part of the vision, but there is no meaningful implementation.

## Planning

The architecture or behavior is designed, but not meaningfully built.

## Prototype

A partial implementation exists, but it is incomplete, fragmented, fragile, or not yet trustworthy enough to be considered done.

## Implemented

The behavior exists, is wired into the app, and can be trusted within clearly defined limits.

## Important Rule

A feature can be implemented in one narrow area and still be Concept elsewhere.

Example:

- Goal funding may be Prototype.
- Goal hierarchy may be Concept.
