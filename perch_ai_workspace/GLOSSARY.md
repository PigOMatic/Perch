# Glossary

## Perch

A Life Operating System focused on clarity.

## Design Bible

The product specification and destination.

## Repository

The current implementation and source of truth.

## Truth Engine

Determines what Perch is allowed to say.

## Priority Engine

Determines what deserves attention.

## Recommendation Engine

Determines what the user may want to do.

## Living World

The system's current model of the user's life and environment.

## Brain

The capture and memory surface for loose life material.

## Capture

A user-entered item such as reminder, waiting item, recurring item, opportunity, event, or goal.

## Read Mode

A calm, passive view that shows what matters without requiring interaction.

## Explore Mode

A deeper view that exposes why something appeared, where it came from, and what supports it.

## Source Lineage

The chain showing where a statement or number came from.

## Confidence

How strongly Perch may present a claim.
