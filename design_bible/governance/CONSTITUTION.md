# The Perch Constitution

## Purpose

Perch exists to give a person clarity over their real life.

It is not a productivity app.
It is not a finance app.
It is not a calendar app.
It is not a habit tracker.

It is a calm, beautifully designed life operating system.

---

## Core Principle

> Clarity, beautifully designed.

Every feature, screen, and decision must serve this principle.

---

## Privacy Rule

Privacy is mandatory.

- User data is private by default.
- Sensitive data should be minimized.
- Analytics improve Perch without exploiting users.

---

## Design Bible Authority

The Design Bible is the architectural contract.
The repository is the permanent source of truth.

---

## Permanent Standard

Every decision should answer:

1. Does this create clarity?
2. Does this reduce mental load?
3. Does this respect privacy?
4. Does this preserve trust?
5. Is this simpler than the alternative?

---

## Closing Statement

Perch is not built to capture a user's life.

Perch is built to help the user see their life clearly.
