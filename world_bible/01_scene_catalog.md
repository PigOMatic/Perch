# Perch Scene Catalog

## Scene Philosophy

Scenes are places in the user's sanctuary. They are not visual skins.

Each scene has a purpose, emotional tone, core objects, audio bed, motion language, and life context.

A scene should answer:

- Why is the user here?
- What kind of life moment does this place support?
- What information naturally belongs here?
- What should disappear because it would break the calm?

## Home Perch / Cabin Desk

**Purpose:** Daily orientation and one-minute relief.

**Emotional tone:** Warm, grounded, safe, lightly rustic, capable.

**Core objects:**

- notebook or planner
- money envelope
- sticky note
- shift tickets or schedule slips
- coffee mug
- pen
- window light

**Best for:** Today, money safety, next obligation, work schedule, one brain note.

**Audio:** Soft room tone, birds, light wind, optional fireplace.

**Motion:** Coffee steam, dust/light movement, very slight parallax.

## Creek

**Purpose:** Reflection and decompression.

**Emotional tone:** Spacious, quiet, breathing room.

**Core objects:**

- journal
- field notebook
- small stone or marker
- water view

**Best for:** Brain review, waiting items, journaling, emotional reset, low-pressure planning.

**Audio:** Creek water, birds, wind through leaves.

**Motion:** Water movement, leaves, light shimmer.

## Firepit

**Purpose:** Evening review and winding down.

**Emotional tone:** Warm, slower, reflective, family-safe.

**Core objects:**

- firepit
- small table
- notebook
- mug
- family reminder card

**Best for:** End-of-day recap, tomorrow preview, family plans.

**Audio:** Fire crackle, night insects, soft wind.

**Motion:** Fire flicker, ember glow, smoke drift.

## Barn / Workshop

**Purpose:** Projects, property, homestead, hands-on life.

**Emotional tone:** Practical, capable, rugged, momentum-oriented.

**Core objects:**

- clipboard
- workbench
- pencil
- receipts
- parts tray
- project cards

**Best for:** Property tasks, repairs, homestead planning, project status.

**Audio:** Barn ambience, distant animals, shop fan.

**Motion:** Dust, hanging light movement, subtle fan.

## RV Campsite

**Purpose:** Travel mode and family trip orientation.

**Emotional tone:** Adventure, contained calm, road-trip clarity.

**Core objects:**

- campground map
- reservation card
- fuel receipt
- itinerary notebook
- camp mug

**Best for:** Trip route, next campground, check-in time, family activities, fuel/money safety.

**Audio:** Fire, campground ambience, birds, distant kids, light wind.

**Motion:** Fire, lantern flicker, trees.

## Dock / Lake

**Purpose:** Long-view planning and calm decisions.

**Emotional tone:** Open, hopeful, quiet ambition.

**Core objects:**

- journal
- envelope
- small tackle box or wood table
- horizon view

**Best for:** Goals, financial planning, investment property dreaming, future paths.

**Audio:** Water lap, birds, dock creak.

**Motion:** Water, light reflection.

## Nurse Break Room

**Purpose:** Work context without losing the Perch identity.

**Emotional tone:** Low light, practical, protected, night-shift aware.

**Core objects:**

- shift ticket
- badge card
- coffee
- small notebook
- hospital clock

**Best for:** Shift schedule, sleep protection, reminders, work-specific notes.

**Audio:** HVAC, distant hallway, vending hum.

**Motion:** Clock tick, fluorescent hum, cup steam.

## Rainy Window

**Purpose:** Slow planning and emotional settling.

**Emotional tone:** Protected, inward, calm.

**Core objects:**

- window
- notebook
- mug
- blanket edge
- sticky note

**Best for:** Daily reset, low-energy planning, overwhelm reduction.

**Audio:** Rain, soft room tone.

**Motion:** Rain streaks, window light, steam.

## Trail Map

**Purpose:** Navigation through the user's world.

**Emotional tone:** Discovery, ownership, life growth.

**Core objects:**

- illustrated map
- trails
- location markers
- unlocked places
- future paths hinted but not noisy

**Best for:** Switching scenes, showing life expansion, onboarding the world model.

**Rule:** The map replaces theme picker behavior. The user travels through their sanctuary instead of selecting a skin.
