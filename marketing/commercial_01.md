# Commercial Concept 01 — Cabin Morning

## Concept

A quiet cabin morning.

A person sits with coffee beside an open notebook filled with scattered life notes.

A small bird lands on the table, chirps once or twice, then flies away.

The person smiles and sets down the coffee.

The camera moves toward the notebook.

The messy handwriting begins to fade.

Only calm, useful words remain:

> Enjoy your morning.

Then:

> Let's make sense of today.

The notebook slowly transforms into the Perch Today view.

The app is calm, beautiful, and clear.

Final screen:

Perch

Clarity, presented beautifully.

## Feeling

Relief.

Not productivity.
Not AI.
Not a dashboard.

The feeling is:

> I do not have to carry everything in my head right now.
